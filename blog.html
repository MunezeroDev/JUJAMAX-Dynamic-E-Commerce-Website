Jordan One

The Air Jordan 1 debuted in 1986 as the first signature sneaker developed by Nike for Michael Jordan The Peter Moore designed basketball sneakers featured a simple Nike Dunk inspired designs that incorporated the Nike Swoosh and the Jordan Wings logo and featured Nike Air brand. The black and red colourway of the Air Jordan 1 High was famously banned by the NBA for not adhering to the dress code.
